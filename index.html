ABOUT BTS

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan / Beyond the Scene) is a seven-member South Korean group that debuted in 2013 under BigHit Music. Originally starting as an underdog hip-hop act, they have since evolved into one of the most successful pop groups in music history, consistently breaking industry records and bridging global cultures.

  • Billboard Milestones: The first Korean act to earn multiple #1 singles on the Billboard Hot 100 and top the Billboard 200 album chart.
  • Global Impact: Addressed the United Nations General Assembly three times as youth ambassadors, focusing on mental health and self-love.
  • Live Touring: First Korean act to headline and sell out major global stadiums, including Wembley Stadium and Rose Bowl Stadium.

Members

RM (Kim Nam-joon)

Leader & Rapper. The philosophical backbone and main leader of BTS. Known for his deep, lyricism-heavy rap style, he produces much of the group's music and famously taught himself English by watching Friends.

Jin (Kim Seok-jin)

Vocalist - The eldest member, celebrated for his wide vocal range, clean falsettos, and powerful rock-ballad solo tracks. He brings massive charisma and humor to the group's dynamic.

Suga (Min Yoon-gi)

Rapper & Producer - A fiercely respected underground rapper turned global industry producer. He writes and produces hit tracks both for BTS and massive international artists under his solo alias, Agust D.

J-Hope (Jung Ho-seok)

Rapper & Dancer - The group's main dancer and meticulous choreography captain. He blends sharp, high-energy hip-hop rap styles with an incredibly vibrant, street-dance-infused stage presence.

Jimin (Park Ji-min)

Vocalist & Dancer - A formally trained contemporary dancer known for his elegant, gravity-defying choreography and unique, high-register vocals that anchor the group's signature pop sound.

V (Kim Tae-hyung)

Vocalist - Possesses a rare, rich baritone voice that adds a soulful, jazz-and-R&B-inspired texture to the music. He is heavily recognized for his cinematic visuals and love for classical art.

Jungkook (Jeon Jung-kook)

Vocalist & Dancer The "Golden Maknae" (youngest member) who earned his nickname by excelling at center vocals, main dance breaks, and sub-rapping, later dominating global charts with his solo pop debuts.
